Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Where is the project located?

The Emaar India Business Centre is located on Golf Course Extension Road, Sector 61, Gurugram, one of the most sought-after commercial hubs in NCR.

2. What is the RERA registration number?

The project is registered under RERA No. RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/58 of 2017/7(3)/78/2025/20, dated 10.07.2025.

3. What type of spaces are available in this project?

It offers Grade-A commercial office spaces with modern design, sustainable architecture, and integrated retail & F&B spaces at the ground level.

4. How many towers does the project have?

The development comprises two towers – Tower A (launched) and Tower B (proposed, subject to approvals).

5. What are the key highlights of the building?
  • G + 26 floors
  • 3-level basement parking
  • IGBC Gold Pre-certified green building
  • Seismic Zone 4 compliant structure
  • High-speed elevators with Destination Control System
  • Daylight-optimised design for energy efficiency
6. What sustainability features are included?
  • Rainwater harvesting & reuse for cooling and flushing
  • Energy-efficient design (up to 12% less energy consumption)
  • Solar PV integration (optional)
  • MERV-grade air filters for improved indoor air quality
7. How is the project connected to key areas of Gurugram and Delhi?
  • Adjacent to Sector 55-56 Rapid Metro Station
  • Easy access to Golf Course Road, SPR, NH-48, Dwarka Expressway, and MG Road
  • Approx. 15–20 mins drive to IGI Airport
8. What amenities are available within the premises?
  • Retail & F&B outlets at ground level
  • Atrium Plaza for exhibitions and events
  • Landscaped outdoor spaces
  • Concierge and modern lobby areas
9. Who is the developer of the project?

The project is being developed by Emaar India, a globally recognized real estate developer.
